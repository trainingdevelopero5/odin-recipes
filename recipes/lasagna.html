Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Mince meat
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the mince meat in the tomato sauce
  2. Line the baking tray with lasagna sheets
  3. Put the sauce with meat over the lasagna sheets
  4. Top with cheese
  5. Place in oven and cook until golden brown
